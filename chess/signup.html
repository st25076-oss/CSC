About Chess

Chess is a game played by two people on a board with 64 squares. Each player has 16 pieces, and the goal is to trap the other player’s king so it can’t move. The game started long ago in India, and over time it spread to other parts of the world, changing a little along the way. Now, it’s played everywhere and is known for helping people think and plan better.
