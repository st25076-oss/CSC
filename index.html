Vietnamese Culture

*Family and Respect

  • Family is central to daily life
  • Elders are respected and often live with younger generations
  • Big festivals like Tet (Lunar New Year) focus on family and honoring ancestors

*Food and Cuisine

  • Popular dishes include pho (noodle soup), banh mi (Vietnamese sandwich), and spring rolls
  • Meals balance sweet, salty, sour, and spicy flavors
  • Different regions have their own special dishes

*Traditions and Arts

  • The ao dai is a traditional outfit that shows beauty and pride
  • Folk music and dance are common during celebrations
  • People are friendly and proud of their culture and history
